# Audio Licenses

No third-party audio files are included. The ambient track is generated reproducibly by `design/generate_audio.py`; short interface cues are synthesized by `web/audio.js`. Both are original project material and remain subject to the repository's copyright terms. They do not sample, reproduce or redistribute an external recording.

This removes autoplay downloads, external tracking and music-licensing uncertainty. Audio starts only after the learner activates it, remembers volume locally and remains fully optional.

Rules:

- Do not use audio extracted from games, films, streams or social posts.
- Prefer original audio or CC0.
- Verify the exact item license at acquisition time; a platform's general reputation is not enough.
- Confirm that redistribution inside a public source repository is permitted.
- Keep a local copy of required attribution/license text when the license requires it.
- The example track names shown in the visual concept are placeholders, not vetted recommendations.
